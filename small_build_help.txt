Usage:

SCRIPT_NAME github_godot_docs.zip [options]

Options:
--content|-c=manual|classes (default = manual): output content
--format|-f=html|epub|pdf (default = html): output format
--quality|-q=low|med (default = low): image compression preset
--max_res|-mr=X (default = 1024): largest image dimension allowed
--auto_overwrite|-ao=true|false (default = false): overwrite folders without asking
--exclude_downloads|-ed=true|false (default = false): exclude _downloads from html builds

Examples:
SCRIPT_NAME godot-docs-master.zip -content=manual -max_res=256
